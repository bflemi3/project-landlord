# Research

Customer-discovery and validation materials for Mabenn.

- [**Entrevista — Imobiliárias**](interview-imobiliarias.html) — bilingual (PT-BR / EN) discovery script for interviewing imobiliárias, property managers, and landlords. Open it on a phone or laptop and read straight through during the conversation.
- [**Entrevista — Proprietários**](interview-landlord.html) — bilingual (PT-BR / EN) discovery script for interviewing landlords: how they manage rentals month-to-month, where the friction is, and whether the problem is worth a product. Open on a phone or laptop and read straight through.
