Tenant-Initiated Landlord Invite

A tenant invites their landlord to join mavenn. The landlord receives a personalized email, signs up, and sets up the property. This is a collaborative growth loop — tenants can pull landlords into the platform.

1 Invite Landlord

Tenant enters landlord's name, email, property address, and an optional personal note. Privacy is respected — tenant's email stays private.

→
2 Invitation Sent

Confirmation with clear next steps. The tenant can see their invitation status from their home dashboard and resend if needed.

→
3 Landlord Email

What the landlord receives — a clear, personal email explaining mavenn with the tenant's note, key benefits, and a prominent CTA to get started.

→
4 Tenant Notified

When the landlord joins, the tenant sees it in their notification center. The connection is automatic — statements can start flowing.
