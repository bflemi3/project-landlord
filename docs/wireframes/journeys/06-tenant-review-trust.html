Journey 6

Tenant Review & Trust

The tenant opens a published statement, reviews charges with transparency indicators, previews source documents, and raises questions about charges they don't understand. Trust is built through clarity.

1 View Statement

Tenant sees full statement with amount due, split context, charge breakdown, and transparency indicators showing how each charge was sourced.

→
2 Charge Detail

Drill into any charge to see source details — provider, billing period, usage, corrections, and responsibility allocation. Access source document directly.

→
3 Source Document

Preview the actual provider bill. Tenant can verify the charge matches the source document. Download available for records.

→
4 Raise a Question

If something doesn't look right, select a reason, add context, and submit. Structured questions replace awkward WhatsApp conversations.

→
5 Submitted + Feedback

Confirmation with clear next steps. Landlord is notified. Trust pulse survey captures whether the statement felt clear and understandable.
